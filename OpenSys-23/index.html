About the event


OpenSys is an amalgamation of multiple events that are designed to give you the best competitive experience all while being fun and enjoyable. Since we are the open source community of CBIT, all the events will be completely free for everyone to participate and you also stand a chance to win exciting cash prizes. See you there!

  • Free for everyone to participate
  • Win exciting prizes

When?

January 31st and February 1st, 2023.

Where?

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute Of Technology, Gandipet.

Cascade Arcade

An open-sourced event for all the design and UI/UX enthusiasts of the college community, who will compete against each other to create CSS templates that match the specified pattern.

  • Participation: Individual Event
  • Date: 31st January 2023
  • Reference Link: CSS Battle

UniCode Labyrinth (UCL)

Based on the game of Scratch, where participants compete against each other to reach the destination from the source in the least number of steps using primitive moves such as move up, down, left and right. The event comprises 3 mazes, participants who complete all three mazes have the added advantage of designing a new maze which will be enforced upon all other upcoming participants. The winners will be decided based on the time taken to solve all the mazes and the complexity of the mazes they have created.

  • Participation: Individual Event
  • Date: 1st February 2023
  • Reference Link: Scratch

Decipher

A scavenger hunt where participants compete against time and each other to decrypt a series of problem messages. Decryption and encryption of messages will be performed based on the list of resources provided to the participants. A team that decrypts the message first can encrypt it using the resources provided making the problems even more challenging and facilitating collaboration among teams.

  • Participation: Team Event (Team Size: 2)
  • Date: 1st February 2023

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can participate?

    The events are open to anyone with a zeal to learn, collaborate, and compete over open-source technologies.

  • Is this event open to beginners?

    Whether you're a beginner, professional, or simply someone who is passionate about open-source software, there's something for everyone at OpenSys.

  • Is there any registration fee?

    No, the events are absolutely free and open to everyone.

  • When do the events begin?

    The events take place over two days, on January 31 and February 1.

  • Eligibility for Merit and Participation Certificates

    All students who register and participate in the event are eligible for the participation certificates. The winners stand a chance to win exciting prizes!
